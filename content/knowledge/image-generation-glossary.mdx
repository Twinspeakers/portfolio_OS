## Purpose

This is a high-density vocabulary bank for image prompts.
Use it as modular prompt clauses that you can mix, swap, and iterate.

## Negative Prompting

What to avoid.

- anatomy issues: extra fingers, fused fingers, missing limbs, duplicated eyes, broken joints, impossible neck angles
- face issues: asymmetrical eyes, melting features, waxy skin, duplicated teeth, deformed ears, flat expression artifacts
- text issues: gibberish lettering, mirrored text, random symbols, warped logos, unreadable signage
- render artifacts: low-res, compression blocks, oversharpen halos, banding, noisy shadows, texture smearing
- composition issues: cropped head, cropped hands, tangent overlap, cluttered focal zone, subject off-frame unintentionally
- lighting issues: clipped highlights, crushed blacks, inconsistent shadows, mixed light direction errors, color cast contamination
- style issues: accidental style mixing, muddy palette, overdone bloom, excessive lens flare, cartoon eyes in photoreal scene
- realism guardrails: no watermark, no signature, no frame border, no random UI overlays, no duplicated people in background
