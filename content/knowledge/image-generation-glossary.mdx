## Purpose

This is a reusable vocabulary bank for image prompts.
Use it to build prompts with more control and better consistency.

## Subject

Defines what the image is about.

- person: portrait, athlete, scientist, child, elder
- character traits: confident, exhausted, futuristic, mystical
- object: vintage camera, mech suit, ancient book
- creature: wolf, dragon, jellyfish, hybrid biomech entity
- product: sneakers, smartwatch, packaging mockup

## Scene

Defines where the subject exists.

- environment: forest, city, underwater, desert, lunar base
- era: ancient, contemporary, cyberpunk future
- context: classroom, battlefield, museum, market street
- weather: rainstorm, foggy morning, golden sunset, snowfall

## Composition

Defines layout and framing.

- wide shot, medium shot, close-up, extreme close-up
- centered composition, rule of thirds, asymmetrical framing
- leading lines, foreground framing, negative space
- subject scale: full body, headshot, micro detail

## Lighting

Defines light quality and direction.

- soft diffused light, hard directional light
- rim light, backlight, side light, top light
- cinematic key light + fill light
- volumetric light beams, neon glow, candlelight

## Color

Defines palette and color behavior.

- monochrome, duotone, triadic, complementary palette
- muted earth tones, high-saturation neon, pastel palette
- warm highlights / cool shadows
- color accents: red focal point, teal-orange grade

## Style

Defines the artistic language.

- photorealistic, cinematic, editorial, documentary
- anime, watercolor, oil painting, pixel art, low-poly
- brutalist graphic style, retro poster, fashion campaign
- studio product photography, architectural visualization

## Focus

Defines clarity and depth behavior.

- shallow depth of field, deep focus
- sharp subject, soft background
- bokeh, tilt-shift blur, motion blur
- macro detail focus, tack-sharp texture detail

## Angle

Defines camera viewpoint.

- eye level, low angle, high angle, bird's-eye, worm's-eye
- over-the-shoulder, profile, front-facing, rear view
- Dutch tilt, dynamic diagonal framing

## Text

Defines typography or legible words in image.

- exact phrase: "SUSTAIN THE FUTURE"
- typography style: bold sans-serif, handwritten, stencil
- placement: billboard, poster, shirt print, UI panel
- legibility target: crisp readable lettering

## Mood

Defines emotional direction.

- hopeful, tense, melancholic, triumphant, eerie, calm
- energetic, contemplative, ominous, playful
- emotional contrast: serene scene with subtle unease

## Camera and Lens

Useful for photo-like control.

- lens: 24mm wide, 35mm documentary, 50mm natural, 85mm portrait
- aperture cues: f/1.4 bokeh-heavy, f/8 balanced detail
- sensor feel: full-frame, film grain aesthetic

## Medium and Render Mode

Clarifies output medium.

- digital painting, charcoal sketch, ink illustration
- 3D render, octane render, clay render, isometric render
- analog film still, polaroid photo style

## Materials and Texture

Improves realism and surface quality.

- brushed metal, frosted glass, matte ceramic, worn leather
- wet asphalt, cracked concrete, velvet fabric, translucent skin
- texture cues: weathered, polished, gritty, glossy

## Post-Process and Finish

Defines final image treatment.

- film grain, chromatic aberration, vignette
- bloom, HDR tone mapping, subtle noise
- clean commercial grade, gritty editorial grade

## Negative Prompting

What to avoid.

- low quality, blurry, noisy artifacts
- extra fingers, deformed anatomy, duplicated faces
- text glitches, watermark, logo, oversaturation
- cluttered background, bad perspective

## Output Settings

Useful technical constraints.

- aspect ratio: 1:1, 4:5, 16:9, 9:16
- resolution target: 1024x1024, 1536x1024
- variant count: 4 options, 8 options

## Prompt Blueprint

```md
[Subject], [Scene], [Composition], [Lighting], [Color], [Style], [Focus], [Angle], [Mood], [Camera/Lens], [Materials], [Post-Process], [Text instruction], [Output settings], [Negative prompt]
```

## Example Prompt

```md
A confident engineer presenting a transparent AI dashboard in a clean future lab, medium shot with rule-of-thirds composition, soft directional key light with subtle rim light, cool blue palette with amber accent highlights, cinematic photoreal style, shallow depth of field with sharp subject focus, eye-level camera at 50mm, hopeful and forward-looking mood, brushed aluminum and frosted glass materials, subtle film grain and bloom, include readable text "Build For People", 16:9 aspect ratio, avoid artifacts, extra fingers, blurry text, and watermark.
```
