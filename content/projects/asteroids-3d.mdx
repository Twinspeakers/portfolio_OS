## Goals

- Turn the classic 2D Asteroids game into a fully 3D RPG-style experience.
- Recreate realistic space physics and controls for an immersive gameplay experience.
- Large scale asteroid fields with destructible environments and dynamic lighting.

> Built as a feature to integrate with and expand Fun With Matter's crafting and economy systems.

## Highlights

- Immersive 3D space environment with dynamic lighting and particle effects.
- Customizable spaceship with upgradable weapons and thrusters.
- Element mining for Fun With Matter crafting materials.
- Stunning three-dimensional movements and combat mechanics that stay true to the original Asteroids feel.
