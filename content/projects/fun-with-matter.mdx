## Goals
- Build a story-driven game that combines crafting, exploration, and an evolving economy system.
- A gamified study of chemistry and physics concepts, where players discover and combine elements to create new materials and items.
- Thousands of unique items and crafting combinations that allow for deep experimentation and creativity.

> Based on the Fun With Matter universe

## Highlights

- A vast crafting system with hundreds of base elements that can be combined in unique ways to create new materials, tools, and items.
- A general store where players can buy and sell their creations.
- A combat system that encourages players to experiement with different item combinations to discover powerful synergies and strategies.
- Day/night cycle and stunning visuals that bring the world to life.
- A massive in-game wiki that documents all discovered elements, recipes, and lore, encouraging players to experiment and learn through play.
